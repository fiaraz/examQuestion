0 1 . 1 Give two similarities between a bacterial cell and an animal cell. [2 marks]
0 1 . 2 Give one symptom of salmonella food poisoning (not vomiting or diarrhoea). [1 mark]
0 1 . 3 What is the name of the first antibiotic developed (penicillin)? [1 mark]
0 1 . 4 Suggest why bacteria concentration continued to increase after starting a course of antibiotics. [1 mark]
0 1 . 5 Why did a child feel better after 3 days of antibiotics despite bacteria remaining? [1 mark]
0 1 . 6 Suggest why doctors do not give antibiotics to patients with minor infections. [1 mark]
0 1 . 7 Which part of the blood produces antibodies (white blood cells)? [1 mark]
0 1 . 8 Which part of the blood starts the clotting process (platelets)? [1 mark]
0 2 . 1 Order cell, chromosome, gene and nucleus from smallest to largest. [1 mark]
0 2 . 2 Name the process by which a fertilised egg cell produces specialised cells (differentiation). [1 mark]
0 2 . 3 How many cell divisions are needed to form a 16-cell embryo from a fertilised egg? [1 mark]
0 2 . 4 How many chromosomes will there be in each embryo cell, given 23 pairs in the fertilised egg? [1 mark]
0 2 . 5 Describe what change occurs in the cell during each stage of the cell cycle. [3 marks]
0 2 . 6 Compare the growth of boys with girls using mean height data from birth to 18 years. [6 marks]
0 2 . 7 Give one way cell division by mitosis is important in fully grown animals. [1 mark]
0 3 . 1 Which organs in the human digestive system produce amylase? [1 mark]
0 3 . 2 What was the independent variable in an investigation of pH's effect on amylase activity? [1 mark]
0 3 . 3 Describe how a student would know when all the starch had been digested by amylase. [1 mark]
0 3 . 4 What was the optimum pH for amylase, using a graph? [1 mark]
0 3 . 5 How much time did it take for amylase to digest all the starch, using a graph? [1 mark]
0 3 . 6 Determine the rate of sugar production per minute at 40 seconds. [4 marks]
0 3 . 7 Explain how enzyme molecule structure relates to the effect of pH on amylase activity. [6 marks]
0 4 . 1 Why is light needed for photosynthesis? [1 mark]
0 4 . 2 Which is the correct equation for photosynthesis? [1 mark]
0 4 . 3 Describe how a student should make accurate measurements of the rate of photosynthesis under different light colours. [4 marks]
0 4 . 4 Why are results for two photosynthesis investigations (student and scientist) presented differently? [2 marks]
0 4 . 5 Suggest the range in wavelength of green light, using two graphs. [1 mark]
0 5 . 1 Describe the similarities and differences between benign and malignant tumours. [4 marks]
0 5 . 2 Explain why there are no new cases of skin cancer diagnosed in people younger than 15. [2 marks]
0 5 . 3 Give two conclusions about the number of new skin cancer cases, using data. [2 marks]
0 5 . 4 Suggest why skin cancer incidence data is given per 100,000 population rather than as a total number. [1 mark]
0 5 . 5 Describe two trends shown in skin cancer incidence per 100,000 population data. [2 marks]
0 5 . 6 Calculate the number of males aged 80-84 with skin cancer, given population and incidence rate. [3 marks]
