GCSE Question Search — prototype

Searches real AQA GCSE Physics past-paper questions for a topic. Chemistry/Biology are wired up but have no indexed papers yet.

Data: 3 real AQA GCSE Physics papers (June 2022, June 2023, 2018 specimen), text pulled from AQA's own published PDFs. This is a small demo corpus, not the full past-paper archive.
